# Solving and Proving Inequality 

To prove $f(x) > g(x), \ \forall\ x$ we prove that,
$$(f(x) - g(x))_{min} > 0$$

To prove $f(x) < g(x), \ \forall\ x$ we prove that,
$$(f(x) - g(x))_{max} < 0$$


To solve $f(x) > g(x)$ we can analyse monotonic behaviour of function $h(x) = f(x) - g(x)$.

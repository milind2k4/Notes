### Circle and Semicircles
$$x^{2} + y^{2} = r^{2}$$
is a circle of radius r with centre at (0,0).

$$y = \sqrt{ r^{2} - x^{2} }$$
$$y = -\sqrt{ r^{2} - x^{2} }$$
